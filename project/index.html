Trail of the Month

Mount Si

Location: North Bend, WA

Difficulty: Difficult

Length: 8 miles round trip

Elevation Gain: 3,150 feet

One of the most popular hikes in Washington, Mount Si offers a challenging climb with rewarding views of the Snoqualmie Valley and Seattle skyline on clear days.
