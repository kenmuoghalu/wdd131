Rattlesnake Ledge

Moderate

Location: North Bend, WA

Length: 4 miles round trip

Elevation Gain: 1,160 feet

This popular hike offers stunning views of Rattlesnake Lake and the surrounding mountains. The trail is well-maintained but can be crowded on weekends.

Lake Views Forest Well-Maintained

Mount Si

Difficult

Location: North Bend, WA

Length: 8 miles round trip

Elevation Gain: 3,150 feet

A classic Washington hike that rewards with panoramic views of the Snoqualmie Valley. The trail is steep and rocky in sections, requiring good fitness.

Summit Views Challenging Wildlife

Franklin Falls

Easy

Location: Snoqualmie Pass, WA

Length: 2 miles round trip

Elevation Gain: 400 feet

A family-friendly hike to a beautiful 70-foot waterfall. The trail follows the South Fork Snoqualmie River and is accessible for most skill levels.

Waterfall Family-Friendly River

Lake 22

Moderate

Location: Granite Falls, WA

Length: 5.4 miles round trip

Elevation Gain: 1,350 feet

This trail takes you through old-growth forest to a stunning alpine lake surrounded by peaks. The trail includes boardwalks and rocky sections.

Alpine Lake Old Growth Forest Mountain Views

Mailbox Peak

Difficult

Location: North Bend, WA

Length: 9.4 miles round trip (New Trail)

Elevation Gain: 4,000 feet

A legendary hike known for its steep ascent and the iconic mailbox at the summit. The new trail is longer but less steep than the old route.

Summit Views Iconic Mailbox Very Steep
