WEBSITE PLANNING DOCUMENT

Site Name

PNW Trailhead Guide

  • This name clearly identifies the website's purpose - to serve as a guide to trailheads in the Pacific Northwest region. The name is descriptive, memorable, and directly communicates the site's focus on hiking in this specific geographic area.
  • Optional domain availability: pnwtrailguide.org

Site Purpose

The PNW Trailhead Guide website provides a centralized resource for hiking enthusiasts in the Pacific Northwest. The site will offer detailed trail information including length, elevation gain, difficulty ratings, and directions; a gallery of hiking photos filterable by difficulty level; featured "Trail of the Month" highlights; and an interactive gear checklist generator for trip planning with clear, accessible information for hikers of all experience levels.

Scenarios

Questions asked by the target audience:

  • What are some moderate difficulty hikes within 2 hours of Seattle that are good for a weekend day trip?
  • I'm new to hiking - what essential gear should I bring on my first trail in the Olympic National Forest?
  • Where can I find detailed directions and parking information for the Mount Si trailhead?
